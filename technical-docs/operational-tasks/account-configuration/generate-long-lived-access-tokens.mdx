## Overview

[//]: # "How do I generate long-lived access tokens in My Extole?"

You can generate long-lived access tokens within your My Extole account. These tokens are necessary in order to use Extole's authenticated RESTful APIs.

## Generate Your Token

1. Log in to your My Extole account and navigate to the Security Center.
2. Scroll down to the Access Token section and click the  + New Access Token button.

<img src="https://files.readme.io/70aba35cf468b2653486b56aff5caf307f98f5964f56d50ec04f2177848d78af-4f74f2f-Screen_Shot_2022-11-29_at_4.47.37_PM.png" alt="" />

3. Verify your identity.

<img src="https://files.readme.io/31d6928e5434e058b8dec8cc8a1b45d29e605d7a481a6c56cfd55261f1395339-f869386-Screen_Shot_2022-11-29_at_5.00.13_PM.png" alt="" />

**Step 4**\
Enter a name for your token and click Create.

![](https://files.readme.io/0572d67fdd2f79ae3512fce4fb76d7a597426a582b3adba3db15a7dfcd74da53-59a0842-Screen_Shot_2022-11-29_at_5_01_27_PM.png)

<Warning>
**Save Your Token**


**Please be sure to copy your token and save it for later reference**. Due to security reasons, as soon as you navigate away from this page, you will no longer be able to view this token.
</Warning>
