## Overview

Extole Extensions are designed to add extra functionality to your marketing campaigns, enabling you to enhance performance and achieve specific business goals. With customizable add-ons tailored to diverse needs, Extensions make it easy to expand your campaigns without complex development.

## What Are Extensions?

Extensions are optional tools specifically designed to add advanced functionality to your referral and marketing campaigns. They enable you to go beyond standard capabilities by seamlessly integrating new features into your workflows. 

## Extole Extensions

Leverage Extole Extensions to unlock advanced features. Here are some examples of the Extensions and their capabilities:

### [Reward Bank](https://docs.extole.com/docs/reward-bank)

Allow your customers to accumulate reward values and convert them into tangible rewards. With a centralized Redemption Center, users can browse available rewards, redeem them individually or in batches, and track their redemption history.

### Event Streams

Monitor system events in real-time with advanced search and filtering capabilities, making it easy to diagnose issues, track performance, and ensure seamless event management.  

### Event Validation

Verify the accuracy, consistency, and completeness of event data uploaded through files using a structured validation tool. The tool leverages a configurable schema for precise validation, ensuring reliable and error-free processes tailored to your requirements.

### Create Share Link On Event

Generate shareable links dynamically in response to events. These links can be integrated with your marketing automation platform and used later to enhance engagement and increase campaign participation.

## Getting Started

To start using Extensions:

1. Navigate to the [Partners](https://my.extole.com/integrations) center in your My Extole account.  
2. Go to the **Extensions** tab and click on **+ New Extension**.  
3. Browse the available Extensions and select the ones that align with your campaign goals.  
4. Install and configure the selected Extensions to start enhancing your campaigns immediately.
