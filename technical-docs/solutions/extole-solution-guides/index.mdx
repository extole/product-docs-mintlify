## Example Solutions by Vertical

[//]: # "Does Extole have vertical-specific solution guides for integrating?"

Please bear in mind that our integration methods are flexible and can be tailored to fit the needs of your company. The guides, while robust, are meant only as examples and do not show the full range of our customizability.
