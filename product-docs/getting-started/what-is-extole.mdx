## Extole is Customer-led Growth

[//]: # "What is Extole?"

Extole started as the first software-as-a-service referral platform, and we continue to lead and define the industry of referral marketing. Our clients partner with us to grow. We are not paid search, and we are not loyalty. We are Customer-led Growth.

It is our goal to help you acquire new customers, get more from existing customers, and unlock greater Customer Lifetime Value. We achieve this goal by emphasizing engagement as a key driver of customer quality while offering a lower Customer Acquisition Cost than platforms like Facebook, Google, and Amazon.

Quality is a proxy for customer lifetime value. A high-quality customer is a person who invests in, meaningfully engages with and actively promotes your brand. We specialize in figuring out how to interact with people to get the highest quality customers, and our platform provides you with all the tools necessary to create referral and engagement programs that nurture and leverage authentic customer relationships.

## Success Starts with Referral

[//]: # "What does Extole do?"

Referral marketing is our bread and butter. The Extole platform powers beautiful, on-brand referral programs for the world’s best companies. With Extole, you can quickly integrate referral marketing across your entire customer journey and create advocates everywhere your customers are.

The Extole platform lets your marketers design and launch easy-to-use referral programs. You can also track, test, and optimize your programs; automate reward fulfillment; mitigate fraud; and deliver significant new revenue all through the platform with expert help from your dedicated Extole team. Modern and mobile, Extole-powered Refer A Friend programs are a top source of new customer acquisition and existing customer re-activation.
