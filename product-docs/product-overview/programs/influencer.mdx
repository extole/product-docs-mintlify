## Overview

[//]: # "What is an Ambassador program?"

The goal of an Ambassador program is to recognize your brand’s most influential advocates and treat them differently by giving them an appropriate reward and personal dashboard to view their referral stats and share their offer with friends, family, and followers. The Ambassador program requires no additional technical lift other than what is likely already in place for your Refer A Friend program.

<img src="https://files.readme.io/5b4b411ec625bc5ccee7b400fa6c18402aafcb8530cb5c7e0662235041b0aa85-80123c2-Screen_Shot_2022-06-01_at_11.03.10_AM.png" alt="" width="80%" />

Recognizing ambassadors and incentivizing them to share is done in a number of ways:

* Ambassador are promoted to with the Ambassador Welcome email that emphasizes the exclusivity of the program.
* Ambassadors' share links and codes are personalized, which makes them feel special and important.
* Ambassadors are incentivized to share with their networks en-masse without having to jump through hoops (i.e. the share experience). For example, an ambassador receives the welcome email with their personalized share link and immediately posts it in their Instagram bio. This is quicker and easier than having to generate a link from a share experience on your company's website.
* Ambassadors are rewarded differently (and more) than advocates in a typical Refer A Friend program. For example, ambassadors are rewarded with 20% off coupons, whereas advocates in the evergreen Refer A Friend program are rewarded with 15% off coupons. Once an ambassador generates $250 worth of revenue in friend purchases, they receive a bonus reward.

## Marketing

[//]: # "How do I promote an Ambassador program?"

**Email Promotion**  
Promoting your Ambassador program can be achieved in a variety of ways depending on your company's goals, customer base, and desired flow. You can choose to promote to internally-identified ambassadors and partners using the Ambassador Welcome email, or use Extole Audiences to identify any super advocates from your Refer A Friend program that you'd like to invite.

**Promotion to Apply**  
If you're interested in promoting the Ambassador program more widely, you can leverage the optional Application Experience built into the Ambassador Portal. In this case, your promotional strategy may look similar to that of your Refer A Friend program, except instead of your customers simply signing up, they'll fill out an application to become an Ambassador that you can review and approve.

* Define criteria for what makes a successful ambassador .

  * Send regular nurture emails to keep ambassadors engaged.
  *  Design the invite email so that it is clean and concise to avoid any confusion on how to participate.

## Ambassador Journey

The ambassador journey revolves around the Ambassador Portal. The portal is the hub where ambassadors can engage with your program, starting with the Application Experience if you choose to use one. An ambassador, once you've invited them directly or verified and approved their application, can log into the portal to view their dashboard and to share links and codes with their networks.

<img src="https://files.readme.io/730811726774cbde08e952bc19f90bbd0693aa79b28caeeae4169792b42be97b-cdb6071-Screen_Shot_2022-05-24_at_3.32.04_PM.png" alt="Screen Shot 2022-05-24 at 3.32.04 PM.png" />

Ambassadors to whom you've directly promoted receive the Ambassador Welcome email containing their personal share link or code as well as a link to their dashboard. They can choose to simply copy and paste the share link or code, or they can log into their dashboard to share on web or natively via mobile across a variety of social channels, SMS, or email. Each friend or follower who uses the ambassador's personal link or code will earn the ambassador a reward, which appears in the stats section of their dashboard.

<img src="https://files.readme.io/6ac414fa61d39e2cc46488775228355e9f7627220301a5dcedf7258c89cc77bc-a71a820-Screen_Shot_2022-05-24_at_3.33.02_PM.png" alt="Screen Shot 2022-05-24 at 3.33.02 PM.png" />

## Friend and Follower Journey

Friends may receive share messages directly from your ambassadors via social channels, SMS, or email, encouraging them to sign up, create an account, or make a purchase. Followers, on the other hand, may find and use your ambassadors' personal share links or codes posted on social media.

Once a friend or follower taps on an ambassador's link, they'll be taken to the Friend Landing Experience. This page features the ambassador's name and profile image, delivers any reward earned, and encourages the visitor to shop or sign up, and so on.

## Rules and Rewarding

[//]: # "What rules and rewards are typical for an Ambassador program?"

Each time a friend or follower uses an ambassador's link or code to complete a purchase, the ambassador will earn a reward. These rewards are typically more generous than those offered in a typical Refer A Friend program to both encourage your ambassadors to continuing share with their networks and emphasize the more prestigious nature of being an ambassador. Ambassadors can see how many rewards they've earned, as well as their total reward value, in their dashboard.

## Examples

The Ambassador program isn't one-size-fits-all and doesn't need to involve high-caliber personas on Instagram or TikTok to succeed.

Your "ambassadors" may in fact be:

* Super Advocates
* Influencers  
* VIP Customers
* Affiliates
* Partners  
* Sales Agents

**Athleta**  
Athleta promoted their Ambassador program to fitness instructors. The program streamlined communication with instructors to allowed them to more directly share with their network. Using Extole's performance tracking and custom rewarding made the program efficient and easy to optimize. As a result, the Ambassador program became a primary revenue driver for Athleta.

**Quim**

Quim designed their Influencer program to promote to both sexual wellness doctors and top advocates identified through Extole's platform. The program allowed Quim to track doctors who referred their patients and streamlined communication with doctors. Not having to jump through the hoops of an onsite share experience allowed doctor's to more efficiently share Quim with their networks.
