## Overview

[//]: # "Is Extole ISO 27001 certified?"

Extole is proud to share with our customers that we have received ISO 27001 certification. Published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) and the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC), the ISO 27001 standard sets more than 100 requirements for the creation of a comprehensive information security management system, or ISMS.

## Requirements

These requirements of the certification are related to the physical security of facilities, software product security (from development to delivery environments and processes), financial information, intellectual property, HR processes and personnel, and management's commitment to security work.
