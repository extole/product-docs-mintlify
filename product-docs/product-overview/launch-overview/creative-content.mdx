## Experiences

Extole programs are designed around key experiences in your customers' journey. In a Refer A Friend program, the advocate will encounter the Share Experience when they decide to share an offer, while the friend who receives and clicks on the offer will see the Friend Landing Experience. 

These touchpoints in the journey are meaningful in terms of the actions they support and should be both simple to interact with and visually memorable. Through the templates we provide, a lot of the legwork is already done regarding user experience best practices and accessibility. In other words, you get to focus on the creative aspects that are integral to expressing your brand and engaging your customers.

## Emails

The beautifully designed and well-timed emails that the Extole platform sends are keys to your program success. All Extole program emails boast the following features.

### Consistent, On-Brand Creative

Thanks to Extole’s pre-built creative themes, every email will look great and match the look and feel of your referral program and your brand.

### Mobile-Responsive Designs

Because most customers open and share emails via mobile device, it’s imperative that every email you send looks and works great on mobile. Every piece of Extole creative, including email, is responsive.

### Reliable Delivery

With email, the rubber hits the road at the inbox. An email sent but not inboxed is worthless. Extole’s templates and technology work together to ensure delivery in the user's Primary inbox—not the Promotions tab or Junk. 

### CAN-SPAM Compliance

You can easily engage tens of thousands of your customers through your referral program, so it’s essential that the emails send through your program follow CAN-SPAM requirements by honoring the preferences of everyone who participates.

## Promotions

Any way in which you'd like to promote your Extole programs, we've got you covered. In addition to promotional emails, you can create fully branded marketing placements for your website and mobile app. The Extole platform allows you to build dynamic CTA s in the form of banners, overlays, and more. 

**Common promotional placements for both web and mobile app include**:

* Menu items
* Welcome overlays
* Registration overlays
* Confirmation overlays
* Banners
* Global headers
* Global footers

Each program type we offer at Extole has its own set of best practices for promotion. To learn more about how to promote specific program types, please visit the individual program pages.
