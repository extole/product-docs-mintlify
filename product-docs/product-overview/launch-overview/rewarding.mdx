## Overview

[//]: # "How does Extole rewarding work?"

Our rewards system allows you to add a variety of reward types to your account so they can be distributed through your programs. You can manage individual rewards, add new rewards, see which rewards have been issued, and recognize issues that might compromise your customers’ experience—all within the Rewards section of the Extole platform.

## Types of Rewards

[//]: # "What kinds of rewards does Extole support?"

Extole supports a wide range of reward types to fit the design of your programs and behavior of your customers.

### Single-Use Coupons

Load lists of one-time use coupon codes into the system that can be delivered to your program participants. 

![](https://files.readme.io/f31ff188a148de296be0726e6ebb628a7fef27f2dc8c82b2e7cf6dd3b774b383-ff6362b-Screen_Shot_2022-06-29_at_10.00.57_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-06-29 at 10.00.57 AM.png")

### Account Credits

Reward account holders who participated in your program with account credits in the form of discounts, redeemable points, or cash.

### Charitable Donations

For certain program types or business initiatives, you may want to offer your participants the option to select a charity and make a donation as their reward.

![](https://files.readme.io/f8d6cdab5ad09e28b1dcfca5fd6c054b272c9df6eae8adce0b884e3829293cdb-2848bbd-Screen_Shot_2022-06-29_at_10.10.01_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-06-29 at 10.10.01 AM.png")

### International Rewards

Recognize participants in your programs no matter where in the world they participate from with their currency of choice using our international rewards.

![](https://files.readme.io/f40143c3fb6b5f92c2ee2742d65a3608e4f62ec79ff05d6d9dc08d022cd1ffc1-e3872b1-Screen_Shot_2022-06-29_at_9.19.46_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-06-29 at 9.19.46 AM.png")

### Salesforce Commerce Cloud Automatic Coupon Codes

​​Extole can automatically distribute coupons created in your Salesforce Commerce Cloud platform.

### Gift Cards & Cash-like Rewards

Extole supports the ability to give 100+ different types of gift cards as rewards to your program participants with zero integration. For cash-like options, our top-performing rewards are Visa and Mastercard gift cards.

### Custom Rewards

In cases where you may want to get creative with your rewards, we can work with you to create custom reward types. Custom rewards may include physical gifts, gifts with purchase, loyalty points, or other custom incentives that you or your partners can distribute.
