# Overview

The Acquisition Rate report can show you the change in your Acquisition Rate from week to week. Acquisition Rate measures the odds that a share will result in a transaction, making it a great measure of the overall effectiveness of your friend funnel.

We use the term "transactor" for every *unique* person who transacts *in the specified period*. In your program, the desired outcome may be a download, a booking, or a registration. Whichever event we reward will be the top number in the fraction used to calculate the rate. We only count each person once, so if you have two transaction events, like a registration and a purchase, this will not affect your Acquisition Rate.

Acquisition Rate tends to rise as your program ramps up and then stabilize. You can affect your Acquisition Rate by improving the user experience of your program, personalizing the marketing copy of your friend-side email and landing experiences, or increasing the reward.

## Understanding the Formula

Acquisition Rate = (transactors/advocates) * 100

You can read the Acquisition Rate formula as, "In a given time period, for every **100** people who advocate, I expect **X** unique referred friends to transact," where **X** is your Acquisition Rate. A "transaction" is dependent on your program flow. Common transactions include conversions and registrations. If you have an Acquisition Rate of **16**, for example, that means that for every **100** advocates who share, you can expect **16** unique referred friends to transact. You can [read more about Acquisition Rate here](/guides/getting-help/key-concepts/acquisition-rate).

# Report Output

The data in this report is counted by week. The height of each bar in the visualization represents the Acquisition Rate for that week. To the right of the graph, you'll see your Acquisition Rate for the current week as well as your average Acquisition Rate.

Downloading the raw data from within My Extole, you'll see the following columns:

- **Period start time**: This is the start time of the data in a given week.
- **Period end time**: This is the end time of the data in a given week.
- **Period**: This is how the data in your report is grouped -- in this case, by week.
- **Acquisition Rate**: This is the Acquisition Rate of a given week.

We also have the ability to filter by language and country with this report. For more on our language and country reporting options, please see [this article](/guides/dashboards-and-reporting/configuring-reports/advanced-report-configuration).
