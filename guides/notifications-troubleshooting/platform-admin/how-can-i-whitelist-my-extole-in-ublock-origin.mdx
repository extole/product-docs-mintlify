# Overview

The Ad Block Chrome Extension, uBlock Origin, has added extole.io into the list of sites that it blocks. This does not impact customer-facing programs, since these program run under a branded domain. Unfortunately, this does impact successful access to My Extole. In order to access My Extole, you will need to add extole.com and extole.io to the allowed list.

# How to Whitelist My Extole

uBlock Origin will show up in the list of Chrome Extensions on the upper right portion of your screen.

 ![mceclip0.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421734089/fe1ad38c5af8e192c0c9f1b71258/mceclip0.png?expires=1778306400&signature=7997e06de75e7d6081b316196aed386b442150b607864d5fa0b5a51ccd0a58d3&req=dSQlF859mYFXUPMW3nq%2BgYn9cdyvM0z2TN%2BBMhwbHo9vDQqHr5cNaNhg1qry%0AwrsHTVidMSuvBB3zvf4d2uiHJzA%3D%0A)

Click on the button for the uBlock Origin Chrome Extension and in the dialog that appears, click on the three gears icon.

 ![mceclip1.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421734090/b08289911542418736edbbda2739/mceclip1.png?expires=1778306400&signature=f83fd296785691a589de42bd528dc3f6a97e3b11e40add9f759fbb397fb940ff&req=dSQlF859mYFWWfMW3nq%2BgZ1O4Zaq5AVzNjO1kmuCh4%2FXzXnQs1ofjm4xDmYU%0Asq2U6SCidK1TO%2BqMiHLRSVL6Edo%3D%0A)

This will open the uBlock Origin **Settings** page in Chrome. Inside of the settings page, you'll want to switch to the **Trusted Sites** tab.

 ![mceclip2.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421734087/b19d6e236dd2f88aa85812477d12/mceclip2.png?expires=1778306400&signature=a923b67c80f4696f730e1651b4ea123ff9b9c401abdbef76de546cbe40e09193&req=dSQlF859mYFXXvMW3nq%2BgdR%2FEb2WizPzD2WXG6pQT6U90sTClEku8m%2FaIKDz%0A%2BqfP%2BYsEJcdTP0ToKzS3iuDTle0%3D%0A)

In the **Trusted Sites** tab:

1. Add "extole.com" and "extole.io" as two additional trusted sites into the list
2. Click the "Apply Changes" button

![mceclip3.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421734085/ef867d3810f5ff6e770e7940c0a9/mceclip3.png?expires=1778306400&signature=b68cc2ca0010aec5258b539b30c75ebbe29b6b77740dec221ff8dfaeed61e2cf&req=dSQlF859mYFXXPMW3nq%2BgVfy9noAEOhpjIDUzfN0pLPWrokbDuXgbbFmuWRd%0AfevPM6Kj2y0KoBToa01AFAkxxGE%3D%0A)

You can close the Settings tab and My Extole should work as expected.
