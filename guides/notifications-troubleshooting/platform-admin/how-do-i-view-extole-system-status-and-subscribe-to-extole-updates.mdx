---
title: "How do I view Extole System Status and subscribe to Extole Updates?"
description: "How to subscribe to Extole status updates and view historical uptime."
---

Subscribing to Extole updates is a good way to stay aware of new developments, routine maintenance, and other events that might affect your referral program's functionality. You can subscribe to announcements at [https://status.extole.com](https://status.extole.com) for alerts about important Extole system status changes. Click the blue "Subscribe to Updates" button and select the communication channel(s) to receive updates.

To see our historical Extole Consumer Experience SLA report broken out by day, go here: [https://status.extole.com/uptime](https://status.extole.com/uptime).
