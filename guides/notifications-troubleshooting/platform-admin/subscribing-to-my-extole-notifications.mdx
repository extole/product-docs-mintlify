# Overview

In your My Extole account, you can manage your subscription to in-app notifications. Just click on your account in the bottom left corner to open the menu and select Notifications. On this page, you will see all recent notifications or an empty table if you are not yet subscribed. Click on the Manage Notifications button in the top right to change your subscription.

# Notification Levels

My Extole notifications include four categories: Business Operations, Reports, Technical, and Configuration Changes. Each category has four subscription levels.

# Channels

You will receive all notifications within your My Extole account by default. Navigating to the [Notifications Center](https://my.extole.com/notifications), you can view the details of each notification and optionally snooze them for up to 30 days. If you'd like to additionally receive notifications via email, click on "Channels: in-app", toggle on your email address, and click "Save". You can also set up a webhook to post to Slack whenever you receive a notification of a specific category:

## Scheduled Reports Notifications

Outside of the [Settings](https://my.extole.com/settings) page, you can subscribe to scheduled report notifications by going to the Reports > [Scheduled Reports](https://my.extole.com/reports?#/schedules) page.

1. Find the desired report schedule
2. On the righthand side of the row, open the menu and select Edit.
3. Add your email to the Notify Email(s) field and click Save & Run.

After completing the above steps, the next scheduled report will be delivered to your inbox. Please note that if you change any of the data in the report before clicking Save & Run, the most recent report will be re-delivered to all scheduled report subscribers with the new data.

## Reward Low Balance Alerts

Outside the [Settings](https://my.extole.com/settings) page, you can subscribe to reward low balance alerts from the [Rewards](https://my.extole.com/account-rewards) center by clicking on the "add me" link at the top of the page.

# Unsubscribing

If you wish to unsubscribe from any of the aforementioned notifications, you can either do so from the [Settings](https://my.extole.com/settings) page or by clicking Unsubscribe or "remove me" from any of the specific pages described above.
