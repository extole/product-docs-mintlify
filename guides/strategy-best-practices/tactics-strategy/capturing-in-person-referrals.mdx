# Overview

For credit unions, banks, lenders and other institutions that provide in-person services, it’s essential to make your referral program available not just online, but also at the branch. Extole’s In-Branch Referral Page ensures that staff can easily capture, track, and credit referrals made during face-to-face interactions when a new customer comes into the branch and tells the employee that they were referred.

# What is the In-Branch Referral Page?

The In-Branch Referral Page is an Extole-hosted form designed for employees to register referrals made in-person at your branch or location. It allows your team to record referrals reliably, ensuring both the referrer and referee receive proper recognition and eventual rewards.

# How It Works

1. **Account Opening:** The employee assists the new customer in opening a qualifying account.
2. **Referral Submission:** The employee accesses the In-Branch Referral Page and enters their own details (name, email, branch).
3. **Customer Details:** The employee collects and inputs the new customer’s information and the referring member’s email.
4. **Tracking:** The employee submits the form and the referral is tracked in Extole’s system.
5. **Reward Fulfillment:** Once the new customer completes the qualifying actions, rewards are automatically issued by Extole.

>

> *The In-Branch Referral Page does not send rewards at the point of submission; it simply establishes the referral relationship. Referrals are valid as long as the account was opened within the previous day.*

>

# Key Benefits

> **Eliminate Reward Confusion:**

> Reduce “Where is My Reward?” (WISMR) requests and customer frustration by ensuring referrals are properly credited from the start.

>

> **Increase Referral Participation:**

> Easily attribute more word-of-mouth referrals taking place in person and get rid of pen & paper referrals.

>

> **Gain Actionable Insights:**

> Track referral activity by employee and branch to reveal your best performers and optimize outreach.

>

> **Quality Check Your In-Branch Referrals:**

> Extole runs standard quality checks on online referrals. By adding quality rules to your in-branch forms, we can ensure that those referrals meet the same high standards, instantly.

>
