Extole is a certified under the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Frameworks. You can see the Extole Privacy Shield certification at: [Extole, Inc. Privacy Shield (Active)](https://www.privacyshield.gov/participant?id=a2zt0000000TOFPAA4&status=Active)

As part of Extole's [General Data Protection Regulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Data_Protection_Regulation) ("GDPR") readiness, Extole customers will have a choice to enter into our standard Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") that includes the European Commission-approved Standard Contractual Clauses ("Model Clauses"). If you are an Extole customer and wish to enter into our DPA, please use the following link: [Extole DPA](https://na3.docusign.net/Member/PowerFormSigning.aspx?PowerFormId=e2930ece-2b30-40cb-bf02-2025ba88ebd6)
