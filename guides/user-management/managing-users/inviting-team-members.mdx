# Overview

Once you have completed the Extole registration process and your account has been created, you may invite your team members to join.

# Steps

1. Click the drop-down menu located on the top right corner of your browser window. From the drop-down, select **Settings** *.*  
​

2. In the Team section, click the **+ New Team Member** button. A popup window will appear, enter the email of the person to add to your team and click **Invite**.  
​  
​![Screen_Shot_2019-04-24_at_11.47.11_AM.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421733649/fdd64ad9949c517ab7411f3a4de5/Screen_Shot_2019-04-24_at_11_47_11_AM.png?expires=1778306400&signature=3b2a2b23f5a68ec065e3ed248fb199db24788d205536c53f267150080d93b735&req=dSQlF859nodbUPMW3nq%2BgQNbxsmN5JC4fwA6EGTtca7aTIcIl6qXdV4dkoHB%0A1Z720dQzNosph%2BXawPF8GS7Jjm4%3D%0A)
3. Before gaining access to the platform, the new team member must complete the registration process by following the link in the invitation email and setting a password.
