As your team changes, you will need to remove users who no longer need access to your dashboard. To remove users, follow the steps below.

# Steps

1. Click the drop-down menu located on the top right corner of your browser window. From the drop-down, select ***Settings.** *​  
​![Screen_Shot_2019-04-24_at_11.41.29_AM.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421733645/a6b3f1d78f1a001056da1f7a611e/Screen_Shot_2019-04-24_at_11_41_29_AM.png?expires=1778306400&signature=8b8c0fbeac9fdc0a827d8175207ae7e9aa8f86ce4956c8c29e31c646dc034668&req=dSQlF859nodbXPMW3nq%2BgfqUPJWgyL4gYo8Qcbs4%2FanNtE0lNyjN5P%2Fsl6pC%0AMYmGOGLEEIEd5KhjnaEALP97cLU%3D%0A)
2. In the Team section, click the ***Edit*** button denoted by the pencil icon.  
​  
​![Edit_Button.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421733704/35506509e5a9e54dd48b7ad12956/Edit_Button.png?expires=1778306400&signature=571f8f4f61843c716a5c8462401ef2b51cc991c5f573e6641b6b51966a373d6e&req=dSQlF859noZfXfMW3nq%2BgcmsE1pl0YTwvo%2FhfOD4RYHj%2F00Y88McPb%2BQXwHT%0AVFnjRqmoYY%2F2UzzrklIlxWww8Z4%3D%0A)
3. This will bring up theedit user modal. Click the ***Delete User** * link located on the lower left part of the pop-up window.  
​  
​![Screen_Shot_2019-05-29_at_12.06.20_AM.png](https://extole-5ef307a0e5b1.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/syy27wia/1421733705/e34eb4ed0dafbed30acf1c5c8b03/Screen_Shot_2019-05-29_at_12_06_20_AM.png?expires=1778306400&signature=919c4d52ebbfdea4f8e9abbd3b6d21bbeb8c6bc309f1230f4ccf2dcb96c0a713&req=dSQlF859noZfXPMW3nq%2BgdZjVfNJuBKd28JnajazDsx8CZFJiKp16tMEL%2B6J%0A3qGeA8hnue4aVB4sdF7ckNsEZ%2B8%3D%0A)
4. The link will change to a prompt asking **"Are you sure?"** followed by the options **Yes** or **No.** Click Yes to remove the user.

The user will be deleted instantly and no notification will be sent to the user.
