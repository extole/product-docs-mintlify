# Overview

Extole’s enterprise account capabilities are great for companies that have many sub-brands, agencies supporting multiple clients, and franchise organizations. With enterprise accounts, a central team can access all sub-brand accounts while sub-brand account teams only have access to their single account.

# Key Benefits

**Access Management  
​** Provide levels of access that make sense for your company. Allow marketing or IT leads to view, manage, or make changes to programs across multiple brands in Extole, while maintaining single-account access for individual brand teams.

**Multi-Program Ownership  
​** Manage programs across brands with ease. If you or your team owns programs in multiple Extole accounts, accessing them needs to be quick and easy so you can focus on creative optimizations, performance, and growth.

**Simple and Secure Login  
​** Log in with one email address one time. Users with multi-account access can log in to My Extole using Google or Microsoft SSO. Once logged in, you can easily switch between accounts without ever having to leave the Extole platform.
