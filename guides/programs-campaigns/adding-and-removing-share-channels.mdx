# Overview

Extole supports a variety of share channels including email, SMS, native mobile, and a variety of social media providers. This guide will highlight how to adjust the share channels that are available to your advocates.

# Share Channel Options

- Email
- SMS
- Native Mobile
- QR Code
- Facebook
- X (Twitter)
- Facebook Messenger
- WhatsApp
- LinkedIn (available via Extole Support)

# Updating Share Channels on a Flow Campaign

If you have Extole’s upgraded Flow Builder, your share channels will look like the below. Easily choose from the drop down which share channels you’d like enabled on mobile (Share Options When Native Sharing is Available) and desktop (Share Options When Native Sharing is Not Available)

# What is Native Sharing?

> Native sharing is the built-in share menu on your phone that pops up when you tap a Share button, letting you quickly send something via text, Slack, email, or any other app you use most.
