# Overview

For clients that use Tango gift card rewards, they will receive an email with a link to redeem their digital gift card. This reward email can be customized with your own branding, messaging and choice of reward. Unlike other emails sent from the Extole platform, this template can only be configured by your CSM as it’s sent directly from Tango Card.

# Customizing your Tango Email

The actual reward email design is configured by your CSM in **Tango Rewards Genius**, but you can customize the messaging.

# Editable elements in Tango templates

**Logo / header image (branding)**

- Tango supports branded templates using **custom header images**.
- **Header image requirements**:

  - **650 x 150 px**
  - **.JPEG or .PNG**

#### **Email Sender Name / Subject Line**

- The email will come from **[noreply@tangocard.com](mailto:noreply@tangocard.com),** but you can change the “name” that it comes from. Recommended to do something like \{Company Name\} Referral Rewards

#### **Message content in the template body**

- This copy will be at the top of the email and can include simple HTML styling
- 3,000 character limit

#### **Redemption text and instructions**

- **Not editable.** You cannot change the redemption text or instructions.
- Redemption text is created by the reward distributor and is included automatically based on the reward.

#### **Email Closing**

- Editable copy at the end of the email
- Best practice: add a hyperlink here to your referral program so that advocates and friends can keep on referring.

# Sender information

Rewards are sent from:

- **noreply@tangocard.com**

If recipients report missing emails:

- Ask them to check spam/junk folders
- Confirm they don’t have filters blocking **noreply@tangocard.com**

# Need help customizing your Tango template?

If you want to customize the Tango email template (for example, branded header images, template copy, or dynamic tags), **contact your Extole CSM or Extole Support**. They can help confirm the right setup, ensure the correct **Gift Card Template ID** is used in your campaign.

For more information on Tango Rewards, check out **[How to Reward with Tango Gift Cards](/guides/rewards-management/setting-up-your-rewards/how-to-reward-with-tango-gift-cards)**.
