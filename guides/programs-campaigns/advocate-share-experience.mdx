# Overview

The heart of a referral program is the advocate experience—the call-to-action used by your customers to share your brand and your product with friends, family, and the world. The sharing experience provides advocates with the following options:

1. Native Sharing: Allows advocates to share with their friends via any eligible apps they may have on their phone
2. SMS Sharing: Allows advocates to share with their friends via text
3. Email Sharing: Allows advocates to email their friends with the referral offer
4. Facebook Sharing: Allows advocates to post the referral offer to Facebook
5. Twitter Sharing: Allows advocates to post the referral offer as a Tweet
6. Messenger Sharing: Allows advocates to message their Facebook friends with the referral offer
7. WhatsApp Sharing: Allows advocates to message their WhatsApp contacts with the referral offer
8. Pinterest: Allows advocates to post the referral offer on Pinterest
9. Share Link: Provides advocates with a personal URL that they can share with friends and family, post on their blog, or share through other channels

# Share Experience
