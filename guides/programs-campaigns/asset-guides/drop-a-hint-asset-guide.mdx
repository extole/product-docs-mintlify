# Overview

Get your consumers to share products with their networks that they want to be gifted!

Drop a Hint programs can be great for new product launches, big-ticket items, and can even act as substitutes for registries and wish lists. Launching a Drop a Hint program may lead to a lower return rate of your products (as people will be gifting products they *know* their friends and family members already love!) and a higher conversion rate than your typical site traffic.

Drop a Hint programs are successful both on- and off-season, can give you a pulse on the products your consumers are *sharing* but not necessarily *purchasing*, and are especially interesting when used for specialty (e.g. new, expensive, or even gender-specific) products.

# Assets

[Learn more about image containers and their behaviors here](/guides/programs-campaigns/asset-guides/creative-image-asset-guide).

Here's the info your creative team will need in order to use a Drop a Hint program to capitalize on forms of advocacy outside of referrals:

- **Share Experience**: How the advocate shares the product that they want with a friend. All product metadata is read using Facebook OpenGraph tags.   
​

  - **Product image size**: 216 x 216px – OG tags will pull in the product image where the "Follow my link and get a reward" images are shown below.
  - OG tags will pull in the **product title** where "Men's Shoes" is shown below.
  - You can configure up to two **product labels**, which can be used for things like "Size" and "Color". These will not be pulled in from the product page – the user will have to specify them.
