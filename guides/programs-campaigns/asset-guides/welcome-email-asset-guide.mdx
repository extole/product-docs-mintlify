# Overview

The **Welcome Email** is the first automated email an advocate receives after their **first share** through your referral program. It welcomes them to the program, reinforces the value of participating, and provides the key information they need to keep sharing.

![](https://files.readme.io/06ac01fce41a13358b2528b14dff1caa1320318f4e2c078cf269a0415e89e719-7eefb506_e9091ff2-dc2d-4f10-b9c9-88c626614c7e.png)

# Key Metrics

  * **Open Rate:** This is one of the highest-open-rate messages in the referral lifecycle.

  * **Share rate:** Encourages advocates to share again quickly.  
​




# Best Practices

  * **Make it celebratory** : acknowledge and thank them for sharing.

  * **Reinforce the reward structure** : remind them what they can earn.

  * **Include their unique share link or QR code** : make it easy to copy or share again.

  * **Provide next steps** : explain how to track progress and see their rewards.

In Extole, you can edit:

  * **CTAs:** Invite to share again




# Testing and Optimization

  * **Subject line** : Tone, personalization, length

  * **Headline** : Welcome-first vs. reward-first messaging

  * **Body copy** : Detail level, tone, clarity of next steps

  * **CTA** : Label, placement, number of CTAs

  * **Visuals** : Use of imagery, icons, or banners

  * **Share link display** : Button vs. text, QR code

  * **Timing** : Immediate send vs. delayed




Ongoing testing helps uncover what resonates with your audience and enables you to continually optimize for both the volume and quality of referral traffic.
