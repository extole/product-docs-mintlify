# Key Activities for Managing your Campaigns

- **[Duplicate a campaign, apply and publish changes](/guides/programs-campaigns/managing-campaigns/making-updates-to-your-campaigns)**
- Update copy & creative in the **[Campaign Editor](/guides/programs-campaigns/campaign-creation-editing/using-extole-s-campaign-editor)**
- **[Schedule](/guides/programs-campaigns/managing-campaigns/pausing-ending-and-scheduling-campaigns#h_afd684d4a1)** a new campaign to go live
- Understand program performance from your **[dashboard](/guides/getting-help/program-and-campaign-dashboards)** as compared to **[benchmarks](/guides/dashboards-and-reporting/extole-dashboards-metrics/how-to-measure-and-benchmark-your-referral-program-success)**
- Run **[recommended reports](/guides/dashboards-and-reporting/recommended-reports)** from the **[Reports](/guides/getting-help/exploring-the-reports-page)** page
- **[Create a promotion link](/guides/getting-help/implementing-your-referral-program/creating-ctas/how-to-create-a-promo-link)** for new CTAs
- **[Upload](/guides/audiences-segmentation/my-audiences/upload-and-email-an-audience)** and **[send emails](/guides/audiences-segmentation/my-audiences/how-to-send-a-promotional-email-to-an-audience)** to Audiences
- **[Investigate a reward inquiry](/guides/rewards-management/reward-fulfillment/how-to-investigate-a-wismr-request)** and **[understand reasons they may not have been rewarded](/guides/rewards-management/reward-fulfillment/wismr-101-understanding-customer-reward-inquiries)**

These basics should help you easily launch new burst and test campaigns, understand how your program is performing, and get the support you need.
