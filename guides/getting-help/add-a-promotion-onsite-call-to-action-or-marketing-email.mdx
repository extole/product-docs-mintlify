# Overview

Promotions, including onsite call-to-actions (CTAs) and email campaigns, are essential for a successful referral program. Use this guide to create, customize, and publish onsite CTAs (such as overlays, headers, or tiles) or send a promotional email via Extole’s upgraded Flow Builder.

# Navigate to the Promotion Viewed Step

#** Add an Onsite Promotion **1. Click on Promotion Viewed and scroll to the Onsite Promotions section.
2. Click + New Content.
3. In the left-hand menu, select your desired CTA format (e.g., overlay, header, tile), then click Add.
4. Enter a custom name for your promotion to help you identify it later.
5. Customize your promotion’s copy and creative:

  1. Directly within Flow Builder, or
  2. By returning to the Assets tab and finding it under the Marketing section.

6. Publish the CTA to your website:

  1. From the main left-hand menu, go to Tech Center → Tag Generator.
  2. Generate and copy the tag for your new CTA.
  3. Add this tag to your website code where you want the promotion to appear.

>

> Need help adding CTAs to your site? See** [Marketing Tags for Marketers](/guides/getting-help/implementing-your-referral-program/creating-ctas/marketing-tags-for-marketers). **>

#** Add a Promotional Email **1. Click on Promotion Viewed and scroll to the Email Promotions section.
2. Click + New Email Promotion.
3. In the left-hand menu, select an email template and click Add.
4. Enter a custom name for your email promotion.
5. Customize your email’s copy and creative:

  1. Directly in Flow Builder, or
  2. By returning to the Assets tab and finding it under the Marketing section.

6. When ready to send your promotional email:

  1. Follow the instructions in** [Upload and Email an Audience](/guides/audiences-segmentation/my-audiences/upload-and-email-an-audience) **.

> Don’t forget, in order to use these CTAs or send the email, your campaign changes must be published.** [Follow this guide](/guides/programs-campaigns/managing-campaigns/making-updates-to-your-campaigns)** to help publish changes and set campaigns live.
