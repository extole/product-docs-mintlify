# Overview

Launching a referral program with Extole is easy, but it requires proper technical implementation. To get your referral experience up and running smoothly for your customers, you'll need to complete a series of technical tasks.

The primary technical requirements for launch are:

1. Adding Extole Javascript tags to power the referral experience
2. Branding your program to match the look and feel of your business
3. Sending events (both online and offline) to track customer activity and issue rewards

Visit our [Extole Solution Guides](https://docs.extole.com/docs/extole-solution-guides) for more information on implementing your referral program.

# Adding Tags

To learn more about adding Extole Javascript tags to your site, visit our [Javascript SDK](https://docs.extole.com/docs/javascript-sdk) guide.
